About Us

The WSU Consulting Club is a dynamic student-founded organization at Washington State University, created by students who are passionate about the consulting industry and committed to helping their peers succeed.


Our club has two major focus areas: Business Consulting and Analytics. We provide a supportive community where students can develop professional skills in these critical areas, gain industry insights, and build meaningful connections that will shape their careers in consulting and beyond.


Whether you're interested in management consulting, strategy, technology consulting, data analytics, business intelligence, or simply want to enhance your problem-solving abilities, our club offers the resources and network to help you thrive.

Business Consulting Focus

Develop expertise in business strategy, operations, and management consulting through real-world case studies and projects

Analytics Focus

Master data-driven decision making, business intelligence, and analytical frameworks essential for modern consulting

Student-Founded

Built by WSU students, for WSU students, with a focus on peer-to-peer learning and support

Our Goals

Continuous Learning

Foster an environment of continuous learning through workshops, guest speakers, case studies, and hands-on consulting projects that develop critical thinking and analytical skills.

Meeting New People

Create meaningful connections with like-minded students, industry professionals, and alumni. Build a strong network that extends beyond the classroom and lasts throughout your career.

New Opportunities

Open doors to internships, job placements, mentorships, and career advancement opportunities in the consulting industry and related fields.

Skill Development

Enhance problem-solving, communication, and strategic thinking abilities through real-world consulting experiences and collaborative projects.

Leadership Growth

Develop leadership capabilities by taking on club responsibilities, leading projects, and mentoring fellow members in their consulting journey.

Industry Exposure

Gain insights into various consulting sectors including management, strategy, technology, and financial consulting through industry partnerships and events.

Background & Mission

Our Mission

The WSU Consulting Club was founded with a clear mission: to bridge the gap between academic learning and professional consulting practice. We believe that every student deserves access to the knowledge, skills, and networks necessary to succeed in the competitive consulting industry.


Our club focuses on three core pillars: connecting students with opportunities, facilitating continuous learning, and building a community where members can meet new people and expand their professional networks. Through these pillars, we aim to empower WSU students to achieve their career aspirations in consulting and related fields.

What Drives Us

Connecting Opportunities

We actively create pathways for members to access internships, job opportunities, and partnerships with leading consulting firms and organizations.

Learning & Growth

Continuous education is at our core. We host workshops, case competitions, and training sessions to ensure members are always developing new skills.

Networking Excellence

Meeting new people and building relationships is fundamental to success. We facilitate connections between students, alumni, and industry professionals.

Student-Centered

As a student-founded organization, we understand the unique challenges and aspirations of WSU students and tailor our programs accordingly.

Collaboration

We believe in the power of teamwork and collaboration, creating an inclusive environment where every member can contribute and learn from others.

Innovation

We constantly evolve our programs and initiatives to stay current with industry trends and provide cutting-edge learning experiences.
